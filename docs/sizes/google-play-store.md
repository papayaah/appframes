# Google Play Store Screenshot Requirements

**Last Updated:** November 24, 2024  
**Source:** Google Play Console

---

## Phone Screenshots

### Requirements
- **Quantity:** 2-8 screenshots (minimum 2 required)
- **Format:** PNG or JPEG
- **File Size:** Up to 8 MB each
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 or 9:16
- **Dimensions:** Each side between 320 px and 3,840 px

### Recommended Dimensions

**Portrait (9:16):**
- 1080 × 1920 pixels (Full HD)
- 1440 × 2560 pixels (2K)
- 1440 × 2960 pixels (Galaxy S8/S9)
- 1440 × 3040 pixels (Galaxy S10)

**Landscape (16:9):**
- 1920 × 1080 pixels (Full HD)
- 2560 × 1440 pixels (2K)

### Notes
- Google Play is more flexible than Apple App Store
- Any dimension within the range is acceptable
- Recommended to use common device resolutions for best display

## 7-inch Tablet Screenshots

- **Format:** PNG or JPEG
- **File Size:** Up to 8 MB each

- **Dimensions:** Each side between 320 px and 3,840 px

**Landscape (16:9):**

## 10-inch Tablet Screenshots

### Requirements
- **Quantity:** Up to 8 screenshots (optional)
- **Format:** PNG or JPEG
- **File Size:** Up to 8 MB each
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 or 9:16
- **Dimensions:** Each side between 1,080 px and 7,680 px

### Recommended Dimensions

**Portrait (9:16):**
- 1600 × 2560 pixels (Nexus 10)
- 2048 × 2732 pixels (iPad Pro equivalent)

**Landscape (16:9):**
- 2560 × 1600 pixels
- 2732 × 2048 pixels

### Notes
- Higher minimum dimension (1,080 px) compared to phone/7" tablet
- Allows for higher resolution screenshots

## Chromebook Screenshots

### Requirements
- **Quantity:** 4-8 screenshots
- **Format:** PNG or JPEG
- **File Size:** Up to 8 MB each
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 or 9:16
- **Dimensions:** Each side between 1,080 px and 7,680 px

**Landscape (16:9) - Most Common:**

## Android XR Screenshots

### Requirements
- **Quantity:** 4-8 screenshots
- **Format:** PNG or JPEG
- **File Size:** Up to 15 MB each (larger than other categories)
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 or 9:16
- **Dimensions:** Each side between 720 px and 7,680 px

### Recommended Dimensions

**Landscape (16:9):**
- 1920 × 1080 pixels (Full HD)
- 2560 × 1440 pixels (2K)
- 3840 × 2160 pixels (4K)

**Portrait (9:16):**
- 1080 × 1920 pixels
- 1440 × 2560 pixels

### Notes
- XR (Extended Reality) includes AR/VR experiences
- Larger file size limit (15 MB) to accommodate high-quality XR screenshots
- Lower minimum dimension (720 px) for flexibility

## Best Practices

### General Guidelines
1. **Use high resolution:** Aim for 1080p or higher for phones
2. **Show actual app content:** No mockups or marketing materials
3. **Localize screenshots:** Provide screenshots in all supported languages
4. **Highlight key features:** Show your app's best functionality
5. **Keep text readable:** Ensure UI elements are clear and legible

### Aspect Ratio Tips
- **16:9 (Landscape):** 1920×1080, 2560×1440, 3840×2160
- **9:16 (Portrait):** 1080×1920, 1440×2560, 2160×3840

### File Optimization
- Use PNG for screenshots with text or sharp edges
- Use JPEG for photographic content
- Compress images to stay under file size limits
- Test on actual devices to ensure quality

### Common Mistakes to Avoid
1. Using incorrect aspect ratios
2. Screenshots too small or too large
3. Including device frames (Google prefers clean screenshots)
4. Excessive text overlay
5. Outdated app version screenshots

## Key Differences: Google Play vs Apple App Store

| Aspect | Google Play | Apple App Store |
|--------|-------------|-----------------|
| Flexibility | Very flexible dimensions | Exact dimensions required |
| Aspect Ratio | 16:9 or 9:16 | Device-specific |
| Minimum Screenshots | 2 (phone only) | Varies by device |
| Device Frames | Not recommended | Acceptable |
| File Format | PNG or JPEG | PNG or JPEG |
| Localization | Optional but recommended | Recommended |
