# Apple App Store Screenshot Requirements

**Last Updated:** November 24, 2024  
**Source:** Apple App Store Connect

---

## iPhone Screenshots

### iPhone 6.5" Display
**Devices:** iPhone 14 Pro Max, iPhone 13 Pro Max, iPhone 12 Pro Max, iPhone 11 Pro Max, iPhone 11, iPhone XS Max, iPhone XR

**Requirements:**
- Up to 3 app previews and 10 screenshots
- Format: PNG or JPEG

**Dimensions (Portrait):**
- 1242 × 2688 pixels
- 1284 × 2778 pixels

**Dimensions (Landscape):**
- 2688 × 1242 pixels
- 2778 × 1284 pixels

## Important Notes

### Screenshot Usage
- Screenshots are **required** for iOS apps
- Only the **first 3** screenshots appear on the app installation sheets
- Screenshots must be in **JPG or PNG** format
- No transparency allowed in screenshots

### Best Practices
1. Use the highest resolution available for your device category
2. Ensure screenshots show actual app functionality
3. Avoid excessive text overlay
4. Use localized screenshots for different markets
5. Keep important content away from device bezels

### File Requirements
- Format: PNG or JPEG
- Color space: RGB
- No alpha channels (transparency)
- File size: No specific limit mentioned, but keep reasonable
